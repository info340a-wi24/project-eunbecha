Problem/Data Domain

In its current landscape, collaborative music consumption doesn't have a lot of great options. Existing systems (e.g. Spotify Jam) are "janky" and hard to navigate, thus discouraging the experience of listening to music with friends. Furthermore, music distribution is often not consistent throughout multiple different platforms due to a multitude of factors. Sometimes artists wonâ€™t push to specific distribution platforms due to copyright etc.. leading listeners to oftentimes have to visit 2-3 sites just to enjoy the music they like.

Our solution to this problem is to create a collaborative playlist webapp that allows multiple people to seamlessly queue music, and the ability to add songs from multiple different platforms.

App Description

Feature 1:

The first feature of our app allows users to access music across multiple different platforms. Our app helps organize and store all the user's favorite songs conveniently in one place. We are implementing a search engine that takes in an input of the song and pulls it from another music application.

Feature 2:

The second feature allows users to share and listen to music with other users simultaneously.
