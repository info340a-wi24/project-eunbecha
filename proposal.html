Problem/Data Domain

In its current landscape, collaborative music consumption doesn't have a lot of great options. Existing systems (e.g. Spotify Jam) are "janky" and hard to navigate, thus discouraging the experience of listening to music with friends. Furthermore, music distribution is often not consistent throughout multiple different platforms due to a multitude of factors. Solution

With the features of our application, we hope to resolve the issues that music enjoyers might have from other streaming platforms. With our application, not only will people be able to have a better experience sharing music with others, but all sharing activities will be located in one place. Additionally, people will find it very useful to find all music in our application. Since some music is exclusive to a streaming service, users will enjoy being able to ignore that exclusivity and experience a cross-platform music streaming service.
